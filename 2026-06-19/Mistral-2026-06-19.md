### **Pros and Cons**
| **Pros**                          | **Cons**                          |
|-----------------------------------|-----------------------------------|

**Verdict:** **Yes, but with guardrails.**
- Prioritize **international cooperation** (e.g., Artemis Accords) over competition.
- Focus on **sustainable** (e.g., reusable rockets) and **equitable** (global access) space programs.
- Avoid **weaponization** and **exploitation** (e.g., lunar land grabs).
